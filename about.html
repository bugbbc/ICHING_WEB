关于 About

我们是谁 Who We Are

易经文明研究院是一所专注于《周易》及其文明意义的独立学术研究机构。

本机构致力于从经典思想出发，重新理解秩序、关系与变化的基本结构，并在此基础上展开对法律、制度与文明形态的系统性研究。

我们并不将《周易》仅视为古代文本或文化遗产，而是将其作为一种具有解释力的思想框架，用以理解人类社会中持续存在的结构性问题。

The I Ching Civilization Institute is an independent academic research institution dedicated to the study of the I Ching and its significance for civilization.

The Institute seeks to reinterpret fundamental structures of order, relationships, and change through classical thought, and to develop systematic research on law, institutions, and civilizational forms.

Rather than treating the I Ching merely as an ancient text or cultural heritage, we regard it as an interpretive framework capable of illuminating enduring structural problems in human society.

我们的研究视野 Intellectual Scope

本机构的研究建立在跨学科与跨文明的基础之上，主要关注以下维度：

The Institute’s research is grounded in an interdisciplinary and cross-civilizational perspective, focusing on the following areas:

  • 经典文本的结构性分析与历史演变研究Structural analysis and historical development of classical texts
  • 传统思想与现代制度之间的关系The relationship between traditional thought and modern institutions
  • 法律、治理与社会秩序的理论基础Theoretical foundations of law, governance, and social order
  • 文明之间的比较研究与相互理解Comparative studies among civilizations and mutual understanding
  • 面向未来社会的不确定性与复杂性问题Issues of uncertainty and complexity in future societies

我们关注的不仅是“文本本身”，更是文本背后的结构逻辑与思想机制。

Our concern extends beyond the text itself to the underlying logics of structure and thought.

我们的基本立场 Intellectual Position

本机构的研究方法具有以下特征：

The Institute’s methodology is characterized by the following principles:

  • 以经典文本为起点，而非终点Beginning with classical texts rather than ending with them
  • 以结构分析为方法，而非单纯解释Employing structural analysis rather than purely interpretive reading
  • 以问题意识驱动研究，而非知识积累Driven by problem-oriented inquiry rather than knowledge accumulation
  • 以跨文明对话为方向，而非单一传统内部阐释Oriented toward cross-civilizational dialogue rather than intra-traditional interpretation

我们强调：经典思想的价值，不在于被保存，而在于被重新激活与再理解。

We emphasize that: The value of classical thought lies not in preservation, but in its reactivation and reinterpretation.

我们的使命 Our Mission

我们的使命是：促进文化的复兴与制度的转型。

Our mission is: to promote cultural renewal and institutional transformation.

这一使命意味着，我们关注的不仅是思想史的延续，更是文化与制度在当代条件下的再组织与再生成。

This mission reflects our commitment not only to the continuity of intellectual traditions, but also to the reorganization and regeneration of culture and institutions under contemporary conditions.

我们的学术实践 Academic Practice

本机构通过多种形式推进研究与交流，包括：

The Institute advances its work through multiple forms of research and engagement, including:

  • 学术研究与学术活动Academic research and scholarly activities
  • 《易经与文明》学术期刊The academic journal I Ching and Civilization
  • 面向青年的持续性研讨机制Sustained seminar-based engagement among young scholars

这些实践共同构成一个以“持续讨论与结构研究”为核心的学术生态，而非一次性活动集合。

Together, these practices constitute an academic ecosystem centered on continuous dialogue and structural inquiry, rather than a collection of isolated activities.
