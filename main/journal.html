期刊方向建议Suggested Editorial Scope

当前内容以学术定位展示为主，后续可直接替换为“投稿须知”“编委会”“在线投稿”等真实模块。The current content focuses on academic positioning and can later be replaced by instructions for authors, editorial board pages, and online submission modules.

Scope I

《周易》与出土文献研究Zhouyi and Excavated Manuscripts

接收围绕版本校勘、异文、简帛材料、图像资料与基础文献整理的学术论文。

Articles may address collation, variants, bamboo and silk manuscripts, visual materials, and documentary foundations.

Scope II

经典互证与思想史研究Classical Comparison and Intellectual History

鼓励《周易》与经学、政治哲学、礼乐传统、医学经典之间的综合研究。

The journal may encourage work linking the Zhouyi with classical studies, political thought, ritual traditions, and medical texts.

Scope III

国际译介与文明比较Translation and Civilizational Comparison

适合刊发海外研究评述、译介论文、文明比较专题及跨学科对话成果。

Ideal for review essays on global scholarship, translation studies, civilizational comparison, and interdisciplinary dialogue.
