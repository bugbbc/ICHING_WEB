Program I

《周易》研究丛书Zhouyi Studies Series

收录文本研究、经典互证、比较研究与专题论文集，形成中心标志性的学术书系。

A flagship series can collect monographs, essay volumes, and comparative research centered on the Zhouyi.

Program II

译介与文献整理系列Translation and Source Edition Series

包括海外研究译著、古籍整理、术语索引与专题文献汇编。

This stream includes global scholarship in translation, annotated texts, terminological guides, and source compilations.

Program III

讲座与对话文集Lecture and Dialogue Volumes

将论坛、讲座和会议成果整理成出版物，形成长期可积累的学术档案。

Lecture series and conferences can be edited into volumes that create a durable intellectual archive.
