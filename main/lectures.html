Series I

《周易》文本与出土材料Zhouyi Texts and Excavated Materials

围绕帛书、简牍、校勘、异文和训诂展开，适合面向经典文献研究者和研究生群体。

Centered on manuscripts, slips, collation, variants, and philology for scholars and graduate students.

Series II

《周易》与中华经典体系The Zhouyi within the Classical Canon

与《尚书》《诗经》《黄帝内经》等经典互证，强调经学结构与文明谱系中的枢纽位置。

The Zhouyi is read with the broader classical canon to illuminate its mediating role in Chinese civilization.

Series III

易学的国际传播与比较诠释Global Transmission and Comparative Interpretation

关注荣格心理学、自然法传统、海外译介与跨语际诠释的历史轨迹。

This series addresses Jungian psychology, natural law, global translations, and comparative interpretive history.
