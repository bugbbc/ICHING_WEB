Forum

年度《周易》与世界文明论坛Annual Zhouyi and World Civilizations Forum

围绕某一年度主题，如“文本、秩序与文明叙述”，组织主旨发言与专题讨论。

A yearly forum can organize keynote talks and panels around a thematic question such as text, order, and civilizational narrative.

出土文献与版本工作坊Workshop on Excavated Materials and Editions

强调材料细读、版本比勘和小规模讨论，适合与高校或研究所联合举办。

A small workshop format suits close reading of manuscripts, editions, and specialized source materials.

PlanningPlanning

会议组织建议Conference Planning Notes

  • 将主论坛、工作坊与学生论坛分层设计，兼顾学术核心与公共参与。Separate the main forum, workshops, and student panels to balance scholarly depth and public reach.
  • 保留双语摘要、嘉宾介绍和成果汇编，便于形成长期档案。Maintain bilingual abstracts, speaker biographies, and proceedings for long-term archival value.
  • 将会议与图书出版、期刊专栏或后续讲座形成联动。Link conferences to book projects, journal special sections, or follow-up lectures.
