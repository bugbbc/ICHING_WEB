讲座Lectures

易经文明研究院将于 2026 年 5 月举办连续三场学术讲座，围绕《周易》文本结构、经典体系关系及跨文明解释展开系统讨论。 The I Ching Civilization Institute will host a three-part academic lecture series in May 2026, focusing on textual formation, classical systems, and cross-civilizational interpretation of the I Ching.

统一时间All Sessions每周三 15:00–16:00Wednesdays, 15:00–16:00
讲座主题Themes文本形成、身心结构、法律思想与文明秩序Textual formation, psycho-structural interpretation, legal thought, and civilizational order
主讲学者Speakers丁四新、张文智、冯象Ding Sixin, Zhang Wenzhi, and Feng Xiang
Series

三场讲座安排Three Lectures

以下安排按照 2026 年 5 月前三个周三依次举行，均为一小时学术讲座。The following lectures are scheduled for the first three Wednesdays of May 2026, each running for one hour.

Lecture 1

出土文献视野下的《周易》文本形成与结构演变The Formation and Structural Evolution of the I Ching in Light of Excavated Texts

丁四新（清华大学）

Ding Sixin (Tsinghua University)

时间：2026 年 5 月 6 日（周三）15:00–16:00

Time: May 6, 2026 (Wednesday) 15:00–16:00

内容简介：本讲座从战国简帛与汉代出土文献出发，重新审视《周易》文本的形成机制与结构演变过程，探讨经传系统如何在历史层累中逐步生成，并揭示其思想结构的文本基础。

Abstract: This lecture re-examines the formation of the I Ching through excavated manuscripts from the Warring States and Han periods, analyzing its textual layering and the historical development of its structural system.

Lecture 2

从《黄帝内经》到荣格心理学：《周易》的身心结构与跨文化解释From the Huangdi Neijing to Jungian Psychology: The Psycho-Structural Interpretation of the I Ching

张文智（山东大学）

Zhang Wenzhi (Shandong University)

时间：2026 年 5 月 13 日（周三）15:00–16:00

Time: May 13, 2026 (Wednesday) 15:00–16:00

内容简介：本讲座以《周易》解释传统为中心，结合《黄帝内经》的身体观与系统思维，以及荣格分析心理学中的原型理论与象征结构，讨论经典文本如何进入身心结构分析与现代心理解释框架之中，并呈现其跨文化阐释的可能路径。

Abstract: This lecture explores the interpretive tradition of the I Ching through the lens of bodily and psychological structures. It connects the system-oriented worldview of the Huangdi Neijing with Jungian analytical psychology, particularly archetypal theory and symbolic structure, to examine how classical texts can be reinterpreted within psycho-structural and cross-cultural frameworks.

Lecture 3

经典解释、法律思想与文明秩序：从《圣经》到《周易》的比较研究Classical Interpretation, Legal Thought, and Civilizational Order: A Comparative Study from the Bible to the I Ching

冯象（清华大学法学院）

Feng Xiang (Tsinghua University Law School)

时间：2026 年 5 月 20 日（周三）15:00–16:00

Time: May 20, 2026 (Wednesday) 15:00–16:00

内容简介：本讲座从法律思想与经典解释学出发，比较《圣经》传统与《周易》解释体系在文明秩序建构中的不同路径，探讨经典文本如何通过解释机制参与法律、伦理与社会秩序的生成。

Abstract: This lecture adopts a legal-philosophical and hermeneutic perspective to compare the biblical tradition with the interpretive system of the I Ching, examining how classical texts participate in the formation of legal, ethical, and civilizational order through mechanisms of interpretation.

Series NoteSeries Note

系列说明Series Note

本系列以《周易》为核心，依次讨论文本形成、身心结构与文明秩序三个层面，构成一个由文献学、解释学到制度与文明比较的连续讨论框架。

The series forms a continuous framework moving from philology and textual formation to psycho-structural interpretation and finally to legal thought and civilizational comparison.
