研究团队 Research Team

易经文明研究院的研究团队由跨学科研究者组成，涵盖哲学、法学、历史学及文明研究等领域。本机构以学术委员会为核心，通过研究员体系与学术网络共同推进《周易》及其文明意义的系统性研究。 The research team of the I Ching Civilization Institute consists of interdisciplinary scholars in philosophy, law, history, and civilizational studies. The Institute is structured around an Academic Council, supported by a system of research fellows and a broader academic network.

研究团队结构 Research Team Structure

研究团队由学术委员会、研究员体系与跨校学术网络构成。 The research team is composed of an Academic Council, Research Fellows, and a broader intercollegiate academic network.

I

学术委员会 Academic Council

学术委员会负责本机构的学术方向指导与研究框架审定，包括重大研究议题的设定与学术标准的把控。

The Academic Council provides intellectual guidance and oversees the Institute’s research framework, including the definition of major research agendas and academic standards.

II

研究员体系 Research Fellows

研究员体系由本机构核心研究人员与合作学者组成，负责具体研究议题的推进与学术成果的产出。

The Research Fellows consist of core researchers and affiliated scholars responsible for advancing specific research agendas and producing scholarly outputs.

  • 张远和（上海海洋大学）Zhang Yuanhe (Shanghai Ocean University)

III III

学术网络（Research Network） Research Network

本机构同时与多所高校及研究者保持合作关系，形成跨校学术交流网络。

The Institute maintains collaborative relationships with scholars and institutions across multiple universities, forming an intercollegiate academic network.

该网络面向具有持续研究兴趣的青年学者与跨学科研究者，支持专题研究与学术讨论的长期开展。

This network is open to emerging scholars and interdisciplinary researchers engaged in sustained academic inquiry and thematic discussions.

组织定位 Institutional Structure

研究院以学术委员会为核心，研究员体系为执行主体，学术网络为开放延展结构，形成稳定而可持续的研究组织方式。

The Institute is structured around an Academic Council at its core, a system of Research Fellows as its operational body, and a Research Network as its open collaborative extension.

  • 学术方向指导 Intellectual guidance
  • 议题推进与成果产出 Research execution and scholarly output
  • 跨校合作与青年参与 Intercollegiate collaboration and emerging scholars
